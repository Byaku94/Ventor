Bio

Venator is a Canadian Groove / Death metal band from Ottawa, Ontario. Venator was formed in the fall of 2012 out of the pure need to create some new and exciting original music.

Combining elements of groove, melody, thrash, as well as progressive elements; Venator seeks to contribute a whole new flavour to metal. What can you expect from Venator?

Powerful, melodic, harsh, bluesy, classy yet thrashy, kick ass heavy metal that compliments itself with wild, loud, and high voltage live performances!
